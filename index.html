Crystal Collection

You are going to start with a random number!

There are 4 crystals to chose from, each click will add a different amount of points specific to each crystal.

You win the game by matching the total score to the random number generated, but you will lose if your score goes over..

The value of the crystals will not be known until you click on them.

Each time the game starts the crystal values will change.

Good luck!
